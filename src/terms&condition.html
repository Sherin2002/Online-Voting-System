Terms And Conditions

Eligibility:

a. The singing competition is open to individuals of all ages.
b. Participants under the age of 18 require parental or legal guardian consent to enter the competition.
c. Employees and immediate family members of the competition organizers are not eligible to participate.


Registration:

a. Participants must register on the competition website and provide accurate and complete information.
b. By registering, participants agree to abide by the rules and regulations of the competition.


Judging and Selection:

a. Entries will be judged based on criteria determined by the competition organizers.
b. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
c. The organizers reserve the right to disqualify any entry that violates the rules or exhibits inappropriate content.


Privacy:

a. Personal information collected during the registration process will be handled in accordance with the competition's privacy policy.
