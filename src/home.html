About Us

"The Voice" is a globally recognized and immensely popular singing competition television series that has captivated
audiences worldwide. Created by John de Mol, a Dutch television producer, "The Voice" first premiered in the Netherlands
in 2010 and quickly gained international acclaim. The format of the show has since been adapted in numerous countries, making it a truly global phenomenon.
